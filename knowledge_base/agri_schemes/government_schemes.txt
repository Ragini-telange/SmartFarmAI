GOVERNMENT AGRICULTURAL SCHEMES & SUBSIDIES – INDIA 2023-24
=============================================================

Compiled for SmartFarm AI knowledge base. Last updated: June 2024.
Source: Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare (MoAFW), GoI.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
1. PM-KISAN SAMMAN NIDHI YOJANA (PM-Kisan)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Scheme Type   : Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
Benefit       : ₹6,000 per year in 3 equal instalments of ₹2,000
Eligibility   : All land-holding farmer families (excluding income tax payers, 
                constitutional post holders, PSU employees, >10,000/month pensioners)
How to apply  : pmkisan.gov.in  |  CSC centres  |  Agriculture dept. offices
Documents     : Aadhaar, Bank account, Land records (Khasra/Khatauni)
Helpline      : 155261 / 011-24300606

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
2. PRADHAN MANTRI FASAL BIMA YOJANA (PMFBY)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Scheme Type   : Crop Insurance
Premium rates :
  - Kharif crops   : 2% of Sum Insured (SI)
  - Rabi crops     : 1.5% of SI
  - Commercial/Hort: 5% of SI
  - Remaining premium: shared between Central & State Governments
Coverage      : Pre-sowing to post-harvest losses (natural calamities, pests, diseases)
How to apply  : Through crop loan at bank; or pmfby.gov.in; or insurance company
                Deadline: Within 7 days of sowing (for standalone policy)
Claim process : Intimation within 72 hours of crop loss to insurance company / 
                Crop Insurance App
App           : "Fasal Bima" app on Google Play / Apple Store
Helpline      : 14447

4. KISAN CREDIT CARD (KCC)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Purpose       : Short-term crop loans at concessional interest
Rate          : 7% per annum (effective 4% with interest subvention for timely repayment)
Limit         : Up to ₹3 lakh short-term; higher for post-harvest/allied activities
Eligibility   : All farmers; tenant farmers; SHG/JLG groups
Documents     : Land records, ID proof, passport photo; can be applied at any bank branch
Benefit       : Also covers accident insurance; ₹50,000 disability + ₹2.5 lakh death

5. PM KRISHI SINCHAYEE YOJANA (PMKSY)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Motto         : "Har Khet Ko Pani, More Crop Per Drop"
Components    :
  a) Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (AIBP) – major/medium irrigation
  b) HKKP – Har Khet Ko Pani (groundwater development, watershed)
  c) WDC-PMKSY – Watershed Development Component
  d) Per Drop More Crop – micro-irrigation (drip & sprinkler)
Subsidy (micro-irrigation):
  - SC/ST/Small/Marginal: 55% on drip, 45% on sprinkler
  - Others: 45% on drip, 35% on sprinkler
Apply through: State Horticulture / Agriculture Department; pmksy.gov.in

6. RASHTRIYA KRISHI VIKAS YOJANA (RKVY)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Type          : Umbrella scheme; capital investment in agriculture
Focus areas   : Infrastructure, food processing, FPO support, agri-startups, mechanisation
RAFTAAR component: Agri-entrepreneurship programme (loans up to ₹25 lakh with 40% grant)
Apply         : State Agriculture Department

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
7. SOIL HEALTH CARD (SHC) SCHEME
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
What          : Free soil testing + customised fertiliser recommendation card
Frequency     : Every 2 years
How           : Collect soil from 0-15 cm depth; submit to nearest Soil Testing Lab
Parameters tested: pH, EC, OC, N, P, K, S, Zn, Fe, Mn, Cu, B
Benefits      : Saves on excess fertiliser; improves soil health; required for FPO loans
Portal        : soilhealth.dac.gov.in

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
8. eNAM – NATIONAL AGRICULTURE MARKET
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
What          : Online trading platform for APMC mandis
Purpose       : Transparent price discovery; reduce middlemen
Participation : Farmer can sell online to any registered trader across India
Current mandis: 1,361 mandis across 23 states (as of 2024)
How to join   : Register at enam.gov.in or through local APMC office
Benefit       : Better price realization; direct payment to bank account

9. PM KUSUM YOJANA (Kisan Urja Suraksha & Utthaan Mahabhiyan)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Purpose       : Solar-powered irrigation pumps; grid-connected solar plants on farm land
Components:
  Component A: 10 GW decentralised ground-mounted solar plants (₹ income to farmers)
  Component B: 20 lakh standalone solar pumps (subsidy: Central 30% + State 30% = 60%)
  Component C: Solarisation of grid-connected pumps (same subsidy structure)
Apply         : State DISCOM / Renewable Energy Department

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
10. FARMER PRODUCER ORGANISATIONS (FPOs)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Target        : 10,000 FPOs by 2024-25
Formation     : Min. 10 farmers to form; registered under Companies Act / Cooperative Act
Benefits per FPO:
  - Equity grant up to ₹18 lakh from NABARD/SFAC
  - Credit guarantee (CGFMU) up to ₹2 crore
  - CBOs/NGOs as Cluster-Based Business Organisations (CBBOs) provide handholding
  - Reduced input costs through collective purchase; better market access
Apply         : SFAC (sfacindia.com), NABARD, State Cooperative Dept.
