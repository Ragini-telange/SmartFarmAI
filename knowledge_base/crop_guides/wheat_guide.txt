CROP CULTIVATION GUIDE – WHEAT (Triticum aestivum)
====================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Wheat is one of India's most important rabi (winter) crops, cultivated across Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, and Bihar.

SUITABLE CONDITIONS
-------------------
- Season       : Rabi (October – March)
- Temperature  : 10–25 °C during growing, 25–30 °C during ripening
- Rainfall     : 250–750 mm annually; irrigated crop preferred
- Soil         : Well-drained loamy or clay-loam soils, pH 6.0–7.5
- Altitude     : Up to 3,300 m (for hill wheat)

POPULAR VARIETIES
-----------------
1. HD-2967  – High yielding, resistant to yellow rust; suitable for IGP plains
2. GW-322   – Gujarat & Rajasthan; drought tolerant
3. K-307    – UP & Bihar; good for late sowing
4. PBW-343  – Punjab & Haryana; early maturing, rust resistant
5. NW-1014  – Madhya Pradesh; high protein content

SOWING CALENDAR
---------------
- Timely sowing  : 1–25 November (ideal)
- Late sowing    : 25 November – 25 December (use late-sowing varieties)
- Seed rate      : 100–125 kg/ha (timely), 125–150 kg/ha (late)

NUTRIENT MANAGEMENT
-------------------
Basal dose (per hectare):
  - Nitrogen (N)  : 120 kg (half basal + half at first irrigation)
  - Phosphorus    : 60 kg P₂O₅
  - Potassium     : 40 kg K₂O

IRRIGATION
----------
Critical stages: Crown Root Initiation (CRI, 20–25 DAS), Tillering, Jointing, Flowering, Milk stage, Dough stage.
6 irrigations recommended for good yield under Punjab/Haryana conditions.

PEST & DISEASE MANAGEMENT
--------------------------
Common Diseases:
1. Yellow Rust (Puccinia striiformis) – yellow stripes on leaves; spray Propiconazole 25 EC @ 0.1%.
2. Brown Rust (P. recondita) – orange pustules; use Mancozeb 75 WP @ 2 g/l.
3. Loose Smut – black powder in ear; use Carboxin 75 WP seed treatment 2 g/kg.

Common Pests:
1. Aphids – use Imidacloprid 17.8 SL @ 0.3 ml/l or Dimethoate 30 EC @ 1.5 ml/l.
2. Termite – pre-sowing Chlorpyrifos 20 EC soil drench 2.5 L/ha.

HARVEST
-------
Harvest when grain moisture is 14–16%. Use combine harvester for large fields.
Average yield: 4–6 tonnes/ha under irrigated conditions.

GOVERNMENT SUPPORT
------------------
- MSP 2023-24: ₹2,275 per quintal
- PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi: ₹6,000/year to eligible farmers
- Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY): crop insurance at 1.5% premium
