CROP CULTIVATION GUIDE – RICE / PADDY (Oryza sativa)
======================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Rice is India's principal Kharif crop and staple food. Major growing states: West Bengal, Uttar Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, Punjab, Odisha, Tamil Nadu.

SUITABLE CONDITIONS
-------------------
- Season       : Kharif (June – November); also Rabi in South India
- Temperature  : 20–35 °C; sensitive to cold below 15 °C
- Rainfall     : 1,000–2,000 mm; or irrigated
- Soil         : Heavy clay or clay-loam with poor drainage; pH 5.0–7.0
- Water        : 4–6 cm standing water during vegetative stage

POPULAR VARIETIES
-----------------
1. Swarna (MTU-7029)     – High yielding; WB, Odisha, UP
2. IR-64                 – Medium duration; Andhra, TN, Karnataka
3. Pusa Basmati-1121     – Long grain aromatic; Punjab, Haryana
4. BPT-5204 (Samba Masuri) – AP, Telangana; export quality
5. Lalat                 – Odisha; flood tolerant

NURSERY & TRANSPLANTING
-----------------------
- Seed rate for nursery : 25–30 kg/ha
- Nursery period        : 25–30 days (short-duration), 30–35 days (long-duration)
- Transplanting spacing : 20 cm × 15 cm (2–3 seedlings per hill)

NUTRIENT MANAGEMENT (per hectare)
----------------------------------
- N: 120 kg (split in 3: basal, tillering, PI stage)
- P₂O₅: 60 kg (full basal)
- K₂O: 60 kg (full basal)
- Zinc sulphate: 25 kg/ha for zinc-deficient soils (common in Punjab, Haryana)

PEST & DISEASE MANAGEMENT
--------------------------
Diseases:
1. Blast (Pyricularia oryzae)      – spray Tricyclazole 75 WP @ 0.6 g/l at boot-leaf stage.
2. Bacterial Leaf Blight (BLB)     – Copper oxychloride 0.25%; use resistant varieties.
3. Sheath Blight (Rhizoctonia)     – Validamycin 3 L @ 2 ml/l at tillering.
4. Brown Plant Hopper (BPH)        – Imidacloprid 200 SL @ 0.25 ml/l.

Insects:
1. Stem Borer  – Cartap Hydrochloride 4G @ 18 kg/ha or light traps.
2. Leaf Folder – Chlorpyrifos 2.5% DP @ 25 kg/ha dusting.
3. Gall Midge  – Carbofuran 3G @ 33 kg/ha at planting.

HARVEST
-------
Harvest at 80% grain maturity (straw turning golden). Moisture: 20–22%. Dry to 14% for storage.
Average yield: 4–7 tonnes/ha (HYV irrigated).

GOVERNMENT SCHEMES
------------------
- MSP Kharif 2023-24: ₹2,183/quintal (common grade)
- PMFBY insurance premium for Kharif rice: 2% of sum insured
- Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY): subsidy on micro-irrigation
- Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY): state-level capital investment
